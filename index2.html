Top Sights in Paris

Discover the most iconic landmarks and attractions in the City of Light.

Landmark

Eiffel Tower

The iconic iron lattice tower is a global cultural icon of France and one of the most recognizable structures in the world.

Museum

Louvre Museum

The world's largest art museum and a historic monument, home to masterpieces like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo.

Landmark

Notre-Dame Cathedral

A medieval Catholic cathedral considered to be one of the finest examples of French Gothic architecture.

Landmark

Arc de Triomphe

Stands at the center of the Place Charles de Gaulle, honoring those who fought and died for France in the French Revolutionary and Napoleonic Wars.

District

Montmartre

A large hill in Paris's 18th arrondissement, known for its artistic history and the white-domed Basilica of the Sacré-Cœur on its summit.

Park

Jardin du Luxembourg

A beautiful garden featuring lawns, tree-lined promenades, flowerbeds, and the picturesque Medici Fountain.
